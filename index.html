Keep Them Blooming

Flower Care Guide

❦

Change the water every two days and add our included flower food sachet. Keep the vase clean to prevent bacterial growth.

Cut 2–3 cm at a 45° angle before placing in water. This increases the surface area for water absorption significantly.

Place your arrangement away from radiators and direct sun. Cool temperatures significantly extend the life of your blooms.

Ripening fruits release ethylene gas which accelerates wilting. Keep flowers in a separate room from your fruit bowl.

While flowers prefer cool air, direct drafts from air conditioning can damage delicate petals. Find a stable, moderate spot.
