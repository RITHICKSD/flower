Our Heritage

A Legacy of Elegance

Founded in the heart of London, Petal & Bloom was born from a singular vision: to treat flowers not just as decoration, but as a medium for emotion and art. What started as a small boutique has grown into a world-renowned name in luxury floristry.

Every stem we select and every bouquet we craft is a testament to our commitment to quality. We collaborate with the world's most prestigious flower farms to ensure that only the most exquisite blooms reach your door.

Isabella Thorne

Founder & Creative Director

Innovation

The Floral Laboratory

At Petal & Bloom, we don't just arrange flowers; we study them. Our dedicated innovation lab works with botanical scientists to extend the life of our blooms and develop new, organic preservation techniques that keep the scent as fresh as the day they were cut.

Molecular Freshness

Using natural nutrients to double the lifespan of cut stems.

Botanical Perfumery

Extracting true floral essences for our bespoke aromatherapy lines.
